# What is Physically Based Rendering

Phyiscally rendering is interested the laws of physics and how that is expressed in maths. The point is to accurately describe the lightings as it would be IRL. If you're not accurately lightings you have a mistake in your rendering algorithms with PBR. We can rely on physics to give us the actual result we are interested.
