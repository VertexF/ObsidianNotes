# Adding weight to rays

It's possible when you are dealing with rays that come back in the direction we care about $L_i(P,\hat{\omega_i})$ (**light indicident radiance**) . You can add weight to the rays which tells the image how much a individual ray contributes to an image. You can for example create a vignet, which is the camera effect based on things getting darker towards the edge of the lens.
