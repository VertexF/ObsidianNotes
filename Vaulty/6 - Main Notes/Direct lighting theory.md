# Direct lighting theory

Even if you have the measured things carefully all the lighting units. You still need able to convert things to make sense for the different light equations. When we are computing direct lighting we are computing the luminance values in our shader for any given point. The final light of that point depends on illumiance $E$ and the **BRDF** or **BSDF**.
$$L_{out} = f(v,l)E$$
