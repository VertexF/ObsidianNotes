# What is ray tracing

This idea that in ray tracing is that send out infinitesimal rays of light through the scene until it interacts with a surface. 

The general idea behind the maths is that you're going integrating a point over the equation of a point from the physics perspective.

When you do ray tracing you are NOT doing rasterization. Meaning that when you do any type of ray tracing you are NOT doing rasterization these are fundimentally different approaches to rendering.
