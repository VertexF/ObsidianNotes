# What is ambient light

Ambient is a way to talk about indirect lighting, if in your scene you have some ambient lighting, you have indirect light. You can work this out with approximations with ambient occlusion, just increasing the base colour lighting or doing some ray tracing to figure out the direct light for a given.

If you're not doing ray tracing, then it's a good idea to do some kind of ambient occlusion or ath the very least increase brightness of your scene, by just adding the bases time by a small value back into your scene.
